Membership

Join a community of passionate Great Dane enthusiasts

Community

Why Join the Lake Shore Great Dane Club?

Membership in the Lake Shore Great Dane Club opens doors to a vibrant community united by a shared passion for one of the most majestic dog breeds. Whether you're a breeder, exhibitor, owner, or simply an admirer of Great Danes, our club offers meaningful connections and opportunities for growth.

Build Community. Connect with fellow Great Dane enthusiasts who understand the unique joys and challenges of living with these gentle giants. Form friendships that extend beyond the show ring into lifelong bonds with like-minded people.

Advance Your Knowledge. Benefit from the collective expertise of our membership. Learn about breed history, genetics, health issues, training methods, and breeding practices from experienced members willing to share their knowledge. Our meetings feature educational discussions and guest speakers.

Show Opportunities. Our annual specialty show is the club's flagship event, and every member plays a role in making it happen. Whether you're beginning your showing journey or are an accomplished exhibitor, being part of the club connects you to the world of AKC conformation.

Mentorship and Support. New members find mentors among our experienced exhibitors and breeders. We believe in giving back to the breed by helping the next generation of Great Dane enthusiasts succeed. Whether it's your first dog or your first show, we're here to help.

Stay Connected. Join our regular Zoom meetings to stay in touch with fellow members, discuss breed topics, and keep up with club news. It's an easy way to be involved no matter where you are in the Chicagoland area.

Have a Voice. Member voting rights mean you help shape the club's future. Participate in deciding priorities, electing leadership, and setting the direction of our organization.

Process

How to Join

1. Attend a Meeting

Join one of our regular membership meetings held via Zoom. Meeting dates are announced through our Facebook page. Guests are always welcome — come meet our members and learn more about the club from the comfort of your own home.

2. Complete an Application

Fill out a membership application. This straightforward form helps us get to know you and your interest in Great Danes. We keep it simple—we want to welcome genuine enthusiasts into our community.

3. Pay Your Dues

Pay your annual dues to complete your membership. For current dues information, attend a meeting or contact us. Payment methods are flexible.

4. Get Sponsored

A current member will sponsor your membership. This is a simple process that strengthens our community by creating personal connections between new and existing members.

5. Board Approval

Your application is reviewed by the board at a regular board meeting. Approval is routine for candidates who meet basic criteria. Once approved, you're officially a member of the Lake Shore Great Dane Club!

6. Start Enjoying Benefits

Welcome to the club! Join our Zoom meetings to connect with the community, build friendships with fellow Great Dane lovers, and volunteer at the annual Specialty Show — the heart of what we do as a club.
