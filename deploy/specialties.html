Annual Specialty Show

Our Showcase of the Breed

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club's annual Specialty Show is our premier event — an AKC-sanctioned conformation show dedicated entirely to the Great Dane. Each year, we celebrate the dogs and owners who exemplify the best of the breed. Browse past results, photos, and highlights below.
