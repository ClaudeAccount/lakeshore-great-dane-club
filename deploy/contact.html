Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own a Great Dane to join the club?

Not at all! While many of our members are Great Dane owners or breeders, we welcome anyone with a genuine interest in the breed. Whether you're considering getting a Great Dane, are a breed enthusiast, or simply appreciate the dog's elegant nature, you're welcome to join our community. Our Associate membership tier is a perfect option for those who are interested but don't yet own a Great Dane.

Can I attend a meeting before joining?

Absolutely! Visitors are always welcome at our Zoom meetings. We encourage prospective members to attend a meeting to meet the club members, ask questions, and get a feel for the community before deciding to join. Meeting dates are announced on our Facebook page — just reach out and we'll share the Zoom link. It's a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere.

How do I enter a specialty show?

To enter a specialty show, your Great Dane must be registered with the American Kennel Club (AKC). Once registered, you can enter through show superintendents using published premium lists. Club members receive entry discounts and can benefit from the club's knowledge about upcoming shows. We're happy to guide you through the process—contact us or attend a meeting to learn more about getting started with showing your dog.

What if I have questions about breeder recommendations?

We're happy to discuss breeder recommendations with you. Responsible breeding is central to our mission. If you're interested in finding a reputable Great Dane breeder, please reach out through this contact form or attend one of our meetings. Our members can share insights about ethical breeders who prioritize health testing, breed standard adherence, and the welfare of their dogs.

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club is an AKC affiliate breed club dedicated to the advancement, education, and celebration of the Great Dane in the greater Chicagoland area.
