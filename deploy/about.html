About the Club

Dedicated to advancing the Great Dane breed and fostering a passionate community of breed enthusiasts

Our Purpose

Our Mission

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club is dedicated to promoting responsible ownership, advancing the Great Dane breed through education and selective breeding, and building a vibrant community of Great Dane enthusiasts throughout the greater Chicagoland area. We are committed to hosting AKC-sanctioned specialty shows, supporting health initiatives, and ensuring that the breed's majestic qualities are preserved for future generations.

As an AKC affiliate, we uphold the highest standards of breed excellence while fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment for everyone who shares our passion for these magnificent dogs.

Our Activities

What We Do

Conformation Shows

We host AKC-sanctioned specialty shows that celebrate Great Dane excellence. Our events attract top breeders and judges, providing opportunities for exhibitors to showcase their dogs while maintaining breed standards.

Breed Education & Health

Through our social media outreach and blog content, we educate the community about responsible breeding, health screening, and proper care of Great Danes. We share breed-specific health resources, owner tips, and educational articles to reach Great Dane enthusiasts everywhere.

Community Events

Beyond shows, we host regular Zoom meetings where Great Dane lovers can connect, share experiences, and build lasting friendships as we grow our community together.

Our Journey

Club History

◆

Our Roots

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club was established by a group of dedicated breed enthusiasts in the Chicagoland area, united by a shared vision of promoting breed excellence and responsible Great Dane ownership.

◆

AKC Affiliation

Achieved official AKC affiliate status (Club #874), enabling us to host sanctioned specialty shows and events that meet the highest breed standards.

◆

GDCA Affiliate

Recognized as an official affiliate of the Great Dane Club of America, connecting our local community to the national parent club and its resources for breed preservation and advancement.

◆

Growing Community

Expanded our programming over the years to include breed education, health initiatives, fun matches, and community events that have strengthened our presence throughout the Chicagoland region.

Today

Continuing Legacy

We remain committed to our founding principles while hosting our annual specialty show, regular Zoom meetings, and embracing modern approaches to breed advancement, health research, and community engagement as a growing club.

Our Partners

Affiliations

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club is proud to be an affiliate of the Great Dane Club of America (GDCA), the national parent club for the breed, and maintains full recognition with the American Kennel Club (AKC). These affiliations ensure that our programs, shows, and initiatives meet the highest standards of breed excellence and integrity.

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club is an AKC affiliate breed club dedicated to the advancement, education, and celebration of the Great Dane in the greater Chicagoland area.
