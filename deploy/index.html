About the Club

A Legacy of Excellence
in the Midwest

The Lake Shore Great Dane Club stands as one of the premier Great Dane breed clubs in the Midwest. As a proud affiliate of the Great Dane Club of America and the American Kennel Club, we are devoted to the preservation, improvement, and celebration of this magnificent breed.

Whether you are a seasoned breeder, a conformation handler, or someone who simply shares a deep love for these gentle giants, you will find a welcoming home with us.
